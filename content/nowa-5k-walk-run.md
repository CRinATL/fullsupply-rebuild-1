## No One Has to Walk Alone

image: nowa-nadia-natasha-arthur.jpg

Our 5th annual Virtual 5k “No One Walks Alone,” also known as NOWA, is something we strongly believe. Many Christian women are seeking a mentor, but they do not know who to ask or where to find one. That’s why Full Supply trains leaders who transforms lives for Christ in the U.S. Russia, India and Africa.

## Join our 5th Annual Virtual 5k Walk/Fun Run

image: nowa-5k-runners.jpg

## Walk or Run with us to Transform Lives

Take your Virtual 5k Walk or Fun Run in your neighborhood, at a local park or on your treadmill. You choose when & where you walk between October 2nd starting at 6:00 a.m. and October 18th ending at 11:59 p.m.

Track your time on an app of your choice. Then be sure to log it on the event website on or before October 20th by 11:59 p.m.
