# About

## About Us
The Lord has used Full Supply to impact thousands of women's lives through its Christ-centered ministry, reaching across the U.S., Europe, Africa, and Asia.

Full Supply is a leadership development ministry focused on mentoring Christian women who are mature in their faith but feel unprepared to confidently mentor others. We provide the training, resources, and support they need to live courageously and fully embrace their calling.

## Our Foundation
The ministry name and calling stem from Paul sending Timothy to "Encourage, Strengthen and Supply what is lacking in the faith" of the Christians so no one would be unsettled by questions or trials they were facing. This produced an unswerving faith that led to greater world impact.

## Vision / Mission / Values
Image: `about-vision-mission-values.jpg`

**Vision**
We envision a day when Christian mentors are easily accessible for every woman who wants one worldwide.

**Mission**
Our mission is to develop Christ-centered mentor leaders by equipping mature Christian women—who may feel unprepared—with the training and resources they need to confidently mentor others. We strive to empower them to live courageously and fulfill their God-given calling with purpose and impact.

**Values**
- **Mentor** – Mentoring develops authentic relationships that expand possibilities
- **Model** – We practice, model and teach women to live christ-centered lives.
- **Multiply** – Multiplying is the best method to make a greater impact in this world.

## Hi, I'm Connie Menser
