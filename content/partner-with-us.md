## Our Philosophy

We believe giving is a ministry to God and others. Therefore we will not urge or pressure someone to give. Rather, we believe people should give freely and graciously. Therefore, we are committed to what is right in the eyes of God and man by fulfilling the Lord’s work in a proper, ethical and honest manner.

## Together We Shape Eternity

Your generosity combined with our cause equals greater kingdom impact. Let’s face it, money is an essential resource that allows us to do what we do best worldwide.

## Frequently Asked Questions

[FAQ ACCORDION]

**How will my donation be spent?**

At Full Supply, we are firmly committed to good stewardship to the funds entrusted to us by our friends and investors given to us transforms a woman’s life.

**Can I afford to make a donation?**

Only you know if you are able to afford to give. However, donations can be as low as $1.00; on the other hand, the sky’s the limit! Full Supply stands by our belief that one should give cheerfully from the heart.

Full Supply mentees are unable to afford the full cost of the Full Supply Mentoring Program. In order to keep the Mentoring Program accessible and to ensure the stability for the ministry, Full Supply invites ongoing support (annually, quarterly or monthly) from faithful partners who will invest in our current and future Christian leaders.

**Can my donation to go a specific campaign?**

Yes. The investor has the ability to designate a contribution to a specific program/campaign.

> “I look forward to writing my check to Full Supply every month because I know it’s going to a good cause.”
