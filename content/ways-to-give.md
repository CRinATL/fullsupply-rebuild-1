### In-Kind
Image: `ways-to-give-in-kind.jpg`
Full Supply appreciates the non-cash donations through goods and services such as computers, office supplies, printing, postage, etc. We are also grateful to volunteers who offer time and/or talent from the daily operations of data entry and mail-outs to administration, marketing/social media, business planning, etc.

### Amazon
Image: `ways-to-give-amazon-smile.png`

Simply use your existing ID and password, select Full Supply, Inc., and make your order. It offers the same products, same prices, and the same Amazon Prime benefits. Start shopping at www.smile.amazon.com for another way to contribute to our cause.
